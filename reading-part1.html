Volunteering at Riverside Museum

The Riverside Museum is looking for new volunteers to help at weekends from September to December. Volunteers welcome visitors, answer simple questions, and support children’s activities in our Learning Room. No previous museum experience is required, but volunteers should enjoy talking to people and feel comfortable helping families.

Shifts run from 9.30 to 13.00 and from 13.00 to 16.30 on Saturdays and Sundays. We ask volunteers to commit to at least two shifts per month. You can choose your own dates using our online calendar, and we are flexible if you need to change at short notice.

All volunteers receive a short training session before their first shift. This covers customer service, health and safety, and how to guide visitors around our most popular galleries. During your first two shifts, an experienced team member will be nearby to support you.

Travel costs up to £6 per day are paid. Volunteers also receive a 15% discount in the café and shop on the days they work. Unfortunately, we cannot offer payment for time, and the role is not suitable for people under 16. To apply, complete the form on our website by 25 August. We will contact selected applicants for a short online chat.
